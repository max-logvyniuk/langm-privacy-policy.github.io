# Privacy Policy — langM

**Last updated:** May 2026

## Overview

langM ("the App") is a vocabulary builder that captures translations from share sheet, and turns them into spaced-repetition flashcards. **All your data stays on your device.** We do not operate any servers that collect, store, or process your personal information.

## What Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally (On-Device Only)

| Data Type         | Purpose                                                   | Shared?  |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| Flashcard content | Your vocabulary cards, review history, and SRS scheduling | ❌ Never |
| App settings      | Your language preferences, notification schedule, theme   | ❌ Never |

This data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device and is **never transmitted** to any external server.

### Data Shared with Third Parties

| Service                                 | Data Shared                                   | Purpose                              |
| --------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Google AdMob** (when ads are enabled) | Device advertising ID, ad interaction data    | Serving and measuring advertisements |
| **RevenueCat** (when Pro is purchased)  | Anonymous app user ID, purchase receipts      | Processing in-app subscriptions      |
| **Google Play**                         | Purchase history, crash reports (if opted in) | App distribution and diagnostics     |

We do **not** share your clipboard content, flashcards, or vocabulary data with any third party.

## Children's Privacy

langM is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

- **Individual cards:** Swipe to delete or use the card editor
- **All data:** Uninstalling the app removes all local data permanently

## Your Rights (GDPR)

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we have no server-side database:

- **Access:** Your data is always accessible within the app
- **Export:** Use **Export** to download all your cards as CSV, JSON, or Anki format
- **Deletion:** Uninstall the app or delete individual cards
- **Portability:** Export your data in standard formats at any time
